About The Developers

Soufiane El Mameri

My name is Soufiane El Mameri, and I'm a 22-year-old aspiring programmer from Morocco. Ever since 2018, I have been deeply passionate about coding. It all began as a hobby when I discovered my love for modding games. Determined to bring my creative ideas to life, I taught myself the programming language C#.

During that time, I was pursuing a degree in business management, but I realized my heart wasn't in it. So, in the following year, I made a life-changing decision to pursue programming as my career. I knew it was the right path for me.

Over the years, I delved into various programming languages, continuously expanding my skill set. While I initially focused on game development, I soon realized that it didn't perfectly align with my long-term goals and aspirations. This realization motivated me to explore other areas of programming.

In 2020, an incredible opportunity presented itself when I became one of the 1 million Arab coders at Udacity. I seized the chance and enrolled in a Full-stack Web Development Course, where I gained invaluable knowledge and hands-on experience. I successfully completed the course and received a well-deserved certificate of completion.

This journey has solidified my passion for coding and confirmed my decision to pursue a career in web development. I am excited to continue learning, growing, and contributing to the ever-evolving world of technology.

Sofiia
